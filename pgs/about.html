Kara Chen

I am a UI/UX Designer and recent Cognitive Science (Design and Interaction) graduate from UCSD!

I like my designs sweet and simple! 😇 I want all users to have an enjoyable experience reaching their goals.

I’m always eager to aqcuire new skills!

To me, life is too short not to learn as much as possible. Nothing excites me more than branching out and finding new ways to apply myself!

Several UI/UX courses and projects have given me experience with user research, user testing, UI design, interaction design, and prototyping. My time with Develop for Good has allowed me to expand my skills in UI/UX design while working with clients to establish the branding and communicate the inspiring stories of their nonprofit organizations.

I am currently learning front-end development and hope to continue to improve in graphic design. I’ve used these skills to develop a website for a family business Sweet O Donuts and design promotional materials for VGDC @ UCSD.

When I’m not designing or developing...

I love traditional / digital painting, photography, gaming, playing the kalimba, cooking / baking, and just enjoying life!
