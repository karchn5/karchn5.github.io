Background

The Video Game Development Club (VGDC) at UCSD is a community dedicated to learning game development skills and creating games together.

As Graphics Chair, I am responsible for the advertising media that is shared with UCSD students to encourage attending events and joining the community. I am also given the opportunity to initiate branding projects such as the development of a club mascot and redesigning the club website.
